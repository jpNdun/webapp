The Story of a
Faithful God

EMCCR community, namely Evangelical Mission Church City of Refuge, was founded by Pastor Alain Tshamala in 2006. Started in Lubumbashi in Congo-Kinshasa, its evolution has since continued in South Portland in the United States where we moved, pushed into this choice by the Holy Spirit.

We are grateful to God, because without his divine intervention, we would never have been able to accomplish this great work. City of Refuge today has 2 extensions, one in Lubumbashi and the other in South Portland. Alain Tshamala is the Lead Pastor and visionary.

Our Mission

Evangelism

We have the noble task according to the commandment of our Lord Jesus Christ to depopulate the devil's kingdom and populate God's Kingdom

Edification

After having gained souls, We baptize them in deep water then we move to the edifying stage, through specific teachings tailored to their need

Training of future leaders

We want to prepare a generation of future leaders and ministers who will continue with spreading the Gospel

Stability in households

We work for the stability and union of households. It is the church's duty to protect families for the development of our community

Our Departments

Choir

Our Choir is a vital component of the worship experience. The choir serves as a spiritual beacon, lifting congregants' hearts and minds heavenward through song.Comprising dedicated singers drawn from the church community, our choir is committed to praise and worship our God.

Children

Held on Sunday mornings alongside regular worship services, Sunday school offers a tailored educational experience designed to engage children at various stages of development. Classes are often divided into age-appropriate groups, ranging from preschoolers to teenagers, allowing instructors to address the unique needs and interests of each age group.

Youth

A youth department in a church plays a pivotal role in fostering the spiritual, social, and emotional growth of young members within the congregation. This department is typically dedicated to serving the needs of teenagers and young adults, providing a supportive community where they can explore and deepen their faith, develop leadership skills, and build lasting relationships.

Pastor Alain Tshamala

Leader of EMCCR

Alain Tshamala is the leader of EMCCR. He is Married to the prophetess Joelle Tshamala and father of one daughter. Alain Tshamala's journey in ministry began with his active involvement in various church activities and leadership roles within his local congregation. His passion for spreading the Word of God and his compassionate nature quickly earned him respect and admiration. In time, Alain was called to serve as the pastor of the Evangelical Mission Church City of Refuge(EMCCR). Under his leadership, EMCCR has flourished and expanded with the main branch being headquarted in the US and one branch in the DR Congo.
In addition to his pastoral duties, Alain Tshamala is a writer. He finds joy and fulfillment in expressing his thoughts and sharing his insights through the written word. His writings often reflect his deep spirituality, his understanding of the scriptures, and his desire to inspire and uplift others. Whether through sermons, articles, or books, Pastor Alain's writing serves as an extension of his ministry, reaching a wider audience and spreading the message of God's love and grace.
